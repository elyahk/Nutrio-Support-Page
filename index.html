FAQ

Common questions

Does Nutrio require an account to use?
No. Nutrio works completely without an account. Just download and start tracking right away.
Does Nutrio collect my personal data?
No. Nutrio does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All your data — meals, goals, history — stays on your device only. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How accurate is the photo food recognition?
Photo recognition works best for common whole foods. For packaged foods or complex meals, we recommend searching by name or using voice logging for the most accurate results.
Can I set custom calorie and macro goals?
Yes. You can set your own daily calorie target and customize your macro goals (carbs, protein, and fat) in the app settings.
What if a food is missing from the database?
If you can't find a specific food, you can add it manually with custom nutrition values. We also continuously expand our food database with updates.
How do I delete my data?
Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete everything by removing the app. No server-side data exists to delete.
